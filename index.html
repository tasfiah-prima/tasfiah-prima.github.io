About Me

I am Tasfiah Tahsin Prima, a dedicated researcher and graduate student in Department of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology at the University of Dhaka. Currently pursuing my M.Sc. degree, under the supervision of Dr Mohammad Riazul Islam at the Molecular Biology Lab. I have a strong academic background with a B.Sc. CGPA of 3.95/4.00, graduating 3rd in my class.

My research interests lie in molecular biology. I have hands-on experience in various laboratory techniques including DNA isolation, PCR, gel electrophoresis, bacterial culture, and cell culture techniques. My recent training in biosafety and animal cell culture has expanded my expertise in BSL-2 laboratory operations.

Beyond academics, I am actively involved in science communication and outreach, having organized the Bangladesh National Science Olympiad and hosted various cultural programs. I am passionate about advancing scientific research and contributing to the field of molecular biology and biotechnology.
